For a full UPC like 023906918347, the calculator uses 91834 and ignores the final check digit 7. If you only enter 5 digits, those 5 digits are treated as the collator number directly. Logic: positions 1–10 use weights 512, 256, 128, 64, 32, 16, 8, 4, 2, 1. Positions 11–17 use weights 64, 32, 16, 8, 4, 2, 1.
